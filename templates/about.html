Storytelling Beyond Genres

I am a storyteller who refuses to be confined by genres. My journey began with a deep, consuming curiosity about the roots of our existence, leading to the creation of the Sanatan Series—an analytical bridge between Vedic wisdom and modern science.

However, the mind is a landscape of both light and shadow. To explore the darker, untested territories of history and human nature, I ventured into the thriller genre with The Vankari Debt.

"Whether decoding the cosmos or unraveling deadly cave mysteries, my goal remains the same: to make you question everything you thought you knew."
