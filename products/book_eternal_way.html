Is Sanatan Dharma merely a collection of archaic rituals, or is it the most sophisticated blueprint for human existence?

"Sanatan: The Eternal Way of Living" by Anuj Rawal is a profound exploration of the timeless philosophy of Sanatan Dharma, decoding its essence through the lens of science and logic. The book bridges the gap between ancient wisdom and modern understanding.

Each chapter unveils the "why" behind practices that sustain both personal well-being and cosmic harmony. Through simple language and scientific parallels, Rawal reveals that Sanatan Dharma is not merely a religion, but an eternal code of life.
