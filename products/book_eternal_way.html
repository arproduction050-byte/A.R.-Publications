About the Book

"Sanatan: The Eternal Way of Living" by Anuj Rawal is a profound exploration of the timeless philosophy of Sanatan Dharma, decoding its essence through the lens of science and logic. The book bridges the gap between ancient wisdom and modern understanding, explaining how rituals, yoga, meditation, fasting, ecology, and festivals are grounded in universal laws of energy and balance.

Each chapter unveils the "why" behind practices that sustain both personal well-being and cosmic harmony. Through simple language and scientific parallels, Rawal reveals that Sanatan Dharma is not merely a religion, but an eternal code of life—a guide for living in alignment with nature's principles.

Readers are invited to rediscover their roots and embrace a holistic lifestyle where spirituality meets rationality. The book serves as both a philosophical reflection and a practical manual for anyone seeking meaning, balance, and inner peace in a fast-changing world.
