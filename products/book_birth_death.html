About the Book

Is Sanatan Dharma just a collection of old traditions, or is it a scientifically designed user manual for human life?

For generations, we have performed rituals because our elders told us to. But in the modern world, "because I said so" is no longer enough. The modern mind demands logic. It asks, "Why?"

• Why do we fast on Ekadashi?
• Why do we wear the Janeu?
• Why is the kitchen placed in the South-East corner?
• Why do we cremate the dead instead of burying them?

In Sanatan: A Journey From Birth Till Death, author Anuj Rawal bridges the gap between the ancient wisdom of the Rishis and the findings of modern science. This book is not about blind faith; it is about decoding the blueprint of human well-being hidden within our traditions.

Inside this book, you will discover the science behind the sacred:

  • The Biology of Fasting: How ancient Vrats trigger Autophagy to cleanse the body of cancer cells and toxins.
  • The Genetics of Gotra: How the prohibition of Sagotra marriage was an ancient method of Genetic Screening.
  • The Neuroscience of Mantras: How Gayatri Mantra stimulates the Vagus Nerve to reduce stress.
  • The Architecture of Energy: How Vastu Shastra aligns your home with the sun’s path and magnetic fields.
  • The Psychology of Rituals: Learn how every Samskara protects physical and mental health.

A Guide for the Modern Seeker: Whether you are a student of science, a devout follower, or a curious skeptic, this book will change the way you look at Indian culture.

Don't just follow the tradition. Understand the truth. Scroll up and grab your copy today.
